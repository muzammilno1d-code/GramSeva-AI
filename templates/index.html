About GramSeva AI

GramSeva AI is a Smart Rural Complaint Management System designed to bridge the communication gap between citizens and local authorities. Residents can report issues such as drainage overflow, street light failures, road damage and water supply problems quickly and efficiently. The goal is to make rural governance more transparent, accessible and digitally empowered.
